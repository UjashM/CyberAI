Whitepaper Guidance

The following must be included with your whitepaper:

  1. Cover Page:
    1. Title of the project
    2. Team Leaders, Team Members, project associates (postdocs and collaborators):
      Please provide full names, title, affiliation, email address, and his/her role in the project.
    3. Timeline of major milestones leading to timely submission of the Team’s proposal.
    4. Funding agency/agencies and program(s) to which the team will apply, application deadline(s), and a link to the program/RFP.
  2. Attach 2-page NSF Compliant CVs of Team Members and Leaders including current grants
  3. Project Whitepaper - not to exceed two pages excluding references.
  4. Budget: Include the following:
    1. Project budget and justification: You may include – expenses for holding a meeting of project members, secretarial help, summer salary.
    2. All members of the Team will need to consent to the proposed budget, and it should be so indicated on the budget page.
    3. Funds may not be used for course buyouts, purchase of food, refreshments, office equipment, computers, software, or conference travel.
    4. The Division for Research will accept requests for external presubmission reviews of selected projects proposals.

Please submit PDF copies of your proposal to: Ms. Theresa Lessard: tlessard@albany.edu

Questions and comments: Directed to Dr. Satyendra Kumar, Associate Vice President for Research, Email: satyenkumar@albany.edu
